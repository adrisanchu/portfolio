Specialised master's programme focused on the back-end of the nuclear fuel cycle: reactor decommissioning strategies, radioactive waste classification and conditioning, geological disposal, and nuclear safety regulation (ASN framework). Industry-facing curriculum with courses delivered by practitioners from EDF, AREVA, CEA, and ANDRA. Fully funded by the Sciences & Enseignement Fellowship awarded by the EDF Foundation and Institut de France on the basis of academic merit. Completed alongside the EDF engineering internship at EDF's nuclear division in Paris.
