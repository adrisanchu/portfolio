- Conducted a techno-economic study of an innovative thermal treatment process for nuclear radioactive waste at EDF DP2D (La Défense)
- Assessed technical feasibility, estimated CAPEX (+100M€) and OPEX (+30M€), sized a 30–50 person industrial facility, and produced a full business plan
- Evaluated risk and opportunities, compared alternative technologies, and modelled lifecycle availability of waste to treat
- Drafted technical and financial reports in French for senior EDF engineering and strategy teams

**Outcome:** The study directly contributed to EDF signing a partnership with Veolia for nuclear waste treatment approximately one year after the internship.
